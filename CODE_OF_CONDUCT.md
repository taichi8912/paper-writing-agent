# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory
comments, publishing others' private information without permission, and other
conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainers through the repository's issue tracker or a
private channel listed in the repository metadata. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
