We address a long-standing problem in deep learning: reducing training latency
without losing accuracy. Our method uses a simpler pipeline to increase
throughput. We tuned the optimizer with a grid search. The method reduced latency
and improved accuracy over the baseline. The improvement was significant
(P = 1.2 x 10^{-5}; Welch's t-test).
